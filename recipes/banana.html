How to make baNANANAz bread !!!!!!!

Step by Step Process:

What's needed. . .

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2(1/3) cups mashed overripe bananas

Now we can start baking!

  1. Prep the oven

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly greased a 9x5 inch loaf

  2. Combination Rally

    3. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separte bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pout batter into prepared loaf pan.

  3. Start the Cooking Process

    4. Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
