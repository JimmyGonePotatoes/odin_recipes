Step by Step Process:

What's needed. . .

  • 1/2 (12 ounce) can CONTADINA Tomato Paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Pinch of

    love

Toppings. . .

  • 1 (6 ounce) package HORMEL Pepperoni
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella chesse, or more to taste
  • Pinch of

    bAnAnAz

    (Optional)

Now we can start baking!

  1. Prepping the Sause

    2. For sause: Combine all sause ingredients with 1/2 cup water in a medium bowl; set aside for flavors to develop while making crust. Freeze remaining paste.

  2. Prepping the Crust

    3. For crust: Combined 2 cups of flour with the dry yeast, sugar and salt. Add the water and oil and mix until well blended (about 1 min). Gradually add enough remaining flour slowly, until a soft, sticky dough ball is formed.

  3. Waiting for the Crust

    4. Knead for about 4 minutes, on a floured surface, until dough is smooth and elastic. Add more flour, if needed. (If using RapidRise Yeast, let dough rest, covered, for 10 minutes.)

  4. Setup for the Oven

    5. Divide dough in half. Pat each half (with floured hands) into a 12-inch greased pizza pan OR roll dough to fit pans.

    Optional: bAnAnAz. . . It's only a prep-food. You gotta eat something big boy/girl!

  5. Cooking Process

    6. For pizzas; Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Top crusts with sauce, pepperoni and chesse.

  6. Wait time for a Complete Pepe-Pizza!

    7. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until crusts are browned and chesse is bubbly. For best results, rotate pizza pans between top and bottom oven racks halfway through baking.
