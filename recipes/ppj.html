Step by Step Process:

What's needed. . .

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons blackberry jelly
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 bananas, frozen and chunked
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons wheat germ

Time to blend!

  1. So EZ!

    2. In a blender combine milk, jelly, peanut butter, banana, honey and wheat germ. Blend until smooth.
